Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Lounja Home.

What is included with my purchase?

You receive access to all current course materials, templates, and any updates released in the future for your chosen plan (Starter, Pro, or Elite).

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. We offer a 14‑day money‑back guarantee from the date of purchase. If the course is not a fit, contact us within 14 days for a full refund.

How long do I have access?

Lifetime access. You can return to the materials and updates any time.

Is prior experience required?

Basic familiarity with web tools is helpful, but the content is designed to be practical and approachable. Pro and Elite include more advanced guidance.

Can I share the course with others?

No. Your purchase grants a single‑user license. Please do not redistribute or share the materials publicly.

How do I receive updates?

Updates are published in the course portal. You will have access to new lessons, templates, and revisions as they are released.
